Research Proposal

How does Data-Driven Innovation affect the environmental performance of organizations?

In order to address the shortcomings of previous research, this research proposal adopts the perspective of dynamic capability theory and proposes a theoretical model. The aim is to explore the impact of DDI on environmental performance in enterprises as well as reveal the mediating role of green innovation and the moderating role of environmental responsibility.
